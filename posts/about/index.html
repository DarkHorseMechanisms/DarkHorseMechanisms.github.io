About

I built this website to store, organise and share all my work for the DarkHorseMechanisms project. This is a creative project meant to house all of my output for professional and personal development. Some of it may not make sense to you, or it may offend you, or you may wonder why I’m doing some or any of it. Nevertheless it is here and you may read it. Some of it is art and I will try to make the distinction clear; when I’m talking about facts or my beliefs, or offering guidance, I am speaking sincerely and sometimes with great urgency. Sometimes I will be trying to leave a legacy of truth for my children. It is inevitable that I will often be wrong.

I’ve kept it relatively anonymous to preserve my ability to speak and create freely without judgement in my personal life and future career. I do welcome discourse.

This website contains a useful link library - selections from my own bookmarks stored here for my use and yours. Almost all of the resources and links are free or contain free content.

This site was made using Hugo and the hermit theme by Track3(License).
